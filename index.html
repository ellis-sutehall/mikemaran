Welcome to
Mike Maran Productions

Mike Maran Productions is more like an extended family rather than an organisation. It is a meeting place where creative people who are all good friends make musical work for the theatre.

'an oasis of real communication
in a desert of media glitz and spin.'

The Scotsman

We don't have a manager, agent or public relations officer. We do however set standards in contemporary British theatre, filling major venues throughout the UK and abroad.

'The audience sat spellbound -
hardly daring to breathe.'

Bath Chronicle

Here you will find out about John Muir: Rhapsody in Green, A Divine Comedy, Indian Peter's Coffee House, Did You Used to be R.D. Laing, Italia 'n' Caledonia, A Funny Valentine, A Christmas Carol, Platero and other Mike Maran Productions. There are pages full of news, reviews, biographies and details of the latest performances.

‘Mike Maran, you have invented
a genre into which you can
cram your whole huge heart.’

The Scotsman

If you have any comments, or you'd like to find out more, don't hesitate to contact us.
