Biography:

David Milligan

David Milligan is one of the most highly regarded pianists in the UK. He worked with Mike Maran on the award winning production, Did You Used to be R.D.Laing? Recently he has developed a unique crossover, merging his talent for jazz with the traditional folk music of his native Scotland. He is a director of the Scottish Big Band phenomenon, The Unusual Suspects.
